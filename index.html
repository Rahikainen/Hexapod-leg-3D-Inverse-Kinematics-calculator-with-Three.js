3D Inverse Kinematics calculator with Three.js

3D motor simulation for one 3 DOF Hexapod leg
with simple trigonometry

Coxa = Hip, Femur = Quad, Tibia = Knee.
Red wireframe spheres present joints (servo motors).
Blue meshes are 3D printed leg parts
and yellow meshes are hobby servo motors.
Red line is the distance vector and red sphere is the destination point.
